## ğŸ› Troubleshooting

**Streak didn't update?**

- Make sure you've achieved your daily goal (not just completed tasks)

- Some achievements require multiple conditions
- Check the Statistics tab to verify your progress
- Achievements unlock automatically when conditions are met

**Quote not changing?**

- Quotes auto-refresh once per day
- Use the refresh button for manual updates

**Daily goal reset?**

- Goals reset at midnight each day
- Previous days' progress is saved in history
